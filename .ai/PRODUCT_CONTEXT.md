# PRODUCT CONTEXT

## Product Vision
SYTU is a premium platform where students, developers, designers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs create portfolios, showcase projects, discover collaborators, and build their professional identity. It acts as a comprehensive Marketplace and Discovery Hub, designed to foster secure, professional connections.

## Core Features
1. **Portfolio Builder:** Tools to showcase skills, experience, and projects.
2. **Project Discovery:** OTT-style browsing patterns to discover what others are building.
3. **Collaboration Hub:** Smart matching to find teammates and co-founders based on shared interests and mutual goals.
4. **Professional Networking:** A secure environment emphasizing mutual consent and verified profiles (No instant anonymous calls).

## Architecture & Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router), React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS (v4), Framer Motion.
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, Socket.IO (planned).
- **Database:** MongoDB (via Mongoose).
- **Infrastructure:** Monorepo-style structure with `/frontend` and `/backend` directories.

## Brand Identity & Aesthetic
- **Vibe:** Premium, Minimal, High-end SaaS, Gen-Z, Professional. (Netflix × Apple × Linear × Dribbble × Notion)
- **Colors:** 
  - Primary: `#B4D2ED`
  - Secondary: `#9CCEEE`
  - Accent: `#A8C3E6`
  - Soft Contrast: `#CBBDDD`
  - Backgrounds: Deep dark hues (`#0f1115`, `#161920`)
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif, large headlines, strong spacing.
- **Visuals:** Soft gradients, glassmorphism (light blur only), smooth hover animations, large cards with depth.
